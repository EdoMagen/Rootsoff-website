Cookies Policy

Last updated: January 11, 2026

1. What are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the website remember your preferences and improve your experience.

2. How We Use Cookies

rooots.ai uses cookies for the following purposes:

  • Essential Cookies: Necessary for the website to function, such as managing your login session and security features (like CSRF protection).
  • Functional Cookies: Remember settings and form preferences to improve onboarding flow.
  • Analytical Cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our website so we can improve the user interface.

3. Third-Party Cookies

We use Google reCAPTCHA which may set cookies to determine if you are a human user. These are necessary for bot protection on our contact form.

4. Managing Cookies

You can manage and delete cookies through your browser settings. Disabling essential cookies may prevent parts of our website from functioning correctly.

5. Privacy Consistency

Cookie usage does not change our core product privacy guarantees: customer code is never used to train models and never stored on disk.
