Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 11, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Roots Off. We are committed to protecting your personal data and your source code. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our Jira RC Analyzer service.

2. Zero-Persistence Architecture

Our core promise is that we do not store your code or your Jira ticket content. Our AI analysis engine works in transient memory:

  • Code is pulled from your VCS (Git/Perforce) for analysis.
  • Analysis happens in a secure, isolated sandbox.
  • Once the analysis result is generated and stored in your Jira instance, all source data is immediately wiped from our buffers.

3. Data Collection

We collect minimal information required to operate the service:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and company name provided during registration or contact.
  • Configuration Data: Settings for your Jira integration and VCS connection.
  • Usage Metrics: Anonymous statistics on the number of tickets analyzed to manage your subscription tier.

4. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encryption of all data in transit via TLS 1.3.
  • Regular security audits and penetration testing.
  • Strict access controls for our internal infrastructure.

5. Third-Party Services

We use a limited number of sub-processors to provide our service:

  • AWS: For infrastructure and secure compute environments.
  • OpenAI/Anthropic: For AI analysis (governed by zero-retention API agreements).

6. Your Rights

Under GDPR and CCPA, you have the right to access, rectify, or erase your personal data held by us. Contact us here for requests.
